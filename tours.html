MAASAI MARA GAME DRIVE

Day 1
We will pick you up from your accommodation in Nairobi around 7am this morning and proceed on the scenic journey towards Maasai Mara. We will descend down the Great Rift valley escarpment on route and there will be time for a quick toilet and shopping stop in Narok. We should arrive at Mara Explorers Camp just outside the Sekenani gate of the Maasai Mara National Reserve in time for lunch. After lunch we will enter the reserve for our first gamedrive of the safari. The Maasai Mara is a Big 5 National Game Reserve meaning it is home to all of the Big 5; Elephant, Rhino, Lion, Leopard and Buffalo. Although some of them are very hard to spot so what you see all depends on your luck on the day! Return to camp for dinner and overnight. L,D

DAY 2
Today we will head out around 7.30am for a full day gamedrive with a packed lunch which we will enjoy later under a shady tree. We will have time to explore large areas of the reserve today and visit an area frequented by Hippos and Crocodiles before we return to camp around 5.30pm today depending on what you see for dinner and overnight. B,L,D

DAY 3
This morning we have the opportunity to do a last early morning gamedrive before we transfer you back to Nairobi to arrive in the late afternoon. B,L

Samburu, Ol Pejeta, Nakuru, Maasai Mara 6 Night 7 Day Road Safari Package

Accomodation:
  • 2x nights Simba Samburu
  • 1x night Sweetwaters
  • 1x night Lake Nakuru Lodge

Day 1
This morning we will depart Nairobi early and head north, crossing the equator, to Samburu National Reserve. We will arrive at our camp around lunchtime and later in the afternoon will head out for our first gamedrive of the safari. L,D

Day 2
Today we have a full day in the Samburu National Reserve. This reserve has many different species of wildlife from those you will fnd elsewhere in Kenya including the Reticulated Giraffe, Grevy's Zebra and Helmeted Guinea Fowl. The climate is also much hotter and arid here. We will return to our camp inside the reserve for dinner and overnight. B,L,D

Day 3
After breakfast we will depart Samburu and head to our camp in the Ol Pejeta conservancy for lunch. The conservancy which we will explore this afternoon is a protected home for Rhino as well as having a Chimpanzee sanctuary. B,L,D

Day 4
We'll depart Ol Pejeta today and head to Nakuru National Park for an afternoon gamedrive. Nakuru is a small national park located around Lake Nakuru and is home to a huge variety of different birdlife. It's also home to both Black and White Rhino as well as the endangered Rothschild Giraffe. B,L,D

Day 5
This morning we will depart Nakuru and begin the journey to the famous Maasai Mara. There will be some rough stretches of road on route but hopefully the destination will be worth it! On arrival we will have lunch at camp before heading out on an afternoon gamedrive. The Maasai Mara is a Big 5 National Game Reserve meaning it is home to all of the Big 5, Elephant, Rhino, Lion, Leopard and Buffalo. Although some of them are very hard to spot so what we see all depends on our luck on the day. B,L,D

Day 6
Today we will have a full day gamedrive in the Maasai Mara National Game Reserve taking in a packed lunch. We will have time to explore large areas of the park and visit an area frequented by Hippos and Crocodiles. B,L,D

Day 7
After breakfast you will have the opportunity to head out on foot with local Maasai guides to stretch your legs on a guided nature walk. Our local Maasai guides will tell you about aspects of their culture including their uses of certain trees and plants in medicine. Afterwards we will transfer you back to Nairobi to arrive in the late afternoon. B,L

Safari price includes all transport and gamedrives in a pop top safari van, full board accommodation with all meals as shown where B=Breakfast, L=Lunch & D=Dinner, all required park entry fees, Maasai guided nature walk. Excludes Flights, visas, unmentioned meals, travel insurance, tips, drinks, snacks, personal shopping, anything else not mentioned above.

14 Day Nairobi - Kigali Safari

Day 1
Arrival into Kigali international airport, meet up with the driver guide. Option to visit the Kigali Genocide memorial and thereafter it’s a 2 hour drive to the Rwanda - Uganda Border. After the border crossing its approx 1 hour to Lake Bunyonyi resort which is our base for the next 2 nights.

Day 2
Gorilla tracking day, approx 2 hours drive to the briefing area and then the trek starts after a short briefing. Gorilla tracking can last from 30 min’s to 4 hours 1 way and once Gorillas are found it’s only an hour spent with them.

Day 3
Drive day to Queen Elizabeth National Park, Afternoon boat game cruise on the Kazinga channel.

Day 4
Morning game drives in Queen Elizabeth National Park and therafter short drive to Kibale forest. Afternoon trek for the chimps

Day 5
Drive day to Jinja, we camp on the edge of the River Nile which runs all the way to Egypt from Lake Victoria.

Day 6
Long Drive day to Kenya and we are heading to Nakuru town which is home to Lake Nakuru National Park.

Day 7
Morning Game drives in Lake Nakuru National park and therafter short drive to Lake Naivasha. Options for game walks or boat rides on lake Naivasha.

Day 8
Drive day to the Maasai Mara, afternoon game drives.

Day 9
Full day’s game drive in the Maasai Mara, options for a hot air balloon safari.

Day 10
Drive day to Nairobi for overnight stay

Day 11
Drive day to Amboseli National park, afternoon game

Day 12
Full day’s Game drive in them Amboseli.

Day 13
Drive day from the Amboseli back to Nairobi

Day 14
Nairobi city tour visiting the Sheldrick’ elephant orphanage, The giraffe center, Kazuri beads, Downtown Nairobi and option for dinner at the carnivores restaurant.
