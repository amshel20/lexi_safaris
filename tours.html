MAASAI MARA GAME DRIVE

Day 1 We will pick you up from your accommodation in Nairobi around 7am this morning and proceed on the scenic journey towards Maasai Mara. We will descend down the Great Rift valley escarpment on route and there will be time for a quick toilet and shopping stop in Narok. We should arrive at Mara Explorers Camp just outside the Sekenani gate of the Maasai Mara National Reserve in time for lunch. After lunch we will enter the reserve for our first gamedrive of the safari. The Maasai Mara is a Big 5 National Game Reserve meaning it is home to all of the Big 5; Elephant, Rhino, Lion, Leopard and Buffalo. Although some of them are very hard to spot so what you see all depends on your luck on the day! Return to camp for dinner and overnight. L,D

DAY 2 Today we will head out around 7.30am for a full day gamedrive with a packed lunch which we will enjoy later under a shady tree. We will have time to explore large areas of the reserve today and visit an area frequented by Hippos and Crocodiles before we return to camp around 5.30pm today depending on what you see for dinner and overnight. B,L,D

DAY 3 This morning we have the opportunity to do a last early morning gamedrive before we transfer you back to Nairobi to arrive in the late afternoon. B,L
